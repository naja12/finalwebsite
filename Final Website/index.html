TOP 10 CONSPIRACY THEORIES

by Naja McCain

1: The "Death" of Tupac

Tupac Shakur. Is he still alive? How did he die? This is one of the most popular conspiracy theories of all time.

2: Avril Lavigne is Dead

Some say Avril Lavigne died in 2003 and is being impersonated by her best friend, Melissa. Read on to see if this theory holds truth.

3: The Mandela Effect

The Mandela Effect is the phenomenon that states that a large number of people believe false memories because of parallel universes that spill over into ours. Read on to find out its origins and its more popular effects.

4: The Moon Landing was Fake

Was the United States really the first country to reach the moon? Or was it an elaborate plan to beat the Soviet Union in the Space Race? This is a widely debated conspiracy with proof on both sides.

5: Michael Jackson was Killed by the Iranian Government

Michael Jackson's real cause of death has been debated since he died. Find out why people think the Iranian government would kill the King of Pop.

6: JFK's Assassination was Planned

It is said that President Kennedy's Assassin, Oswald, was a CIA agent and that it may have been an inside job. Keep reading to find out why the CIA would have a motive to kill the well-liked president.

7: Bruce Lee was Murdered

Many believe that Bruce Lee was too healthy to be killed by a painkiller. Theories include it was the doing of the Chinese mafia or an unknown martial arts master.

8: Stevie Wonder Isn't Blind

Although Stevie Wonder's music has been a joy to many generations, some believe that he is faking his blindness.

9: OJ Simpson is Khloe Kardashian's Real Father

Rumors that Rob Kardashian began circulating after an episdoe of Keeping up with the Kardashians. Find out how this might be possible.

10: Pokemon Go is a Government Spying Program

Is Pokemon Go an innocent, interactive Nintendo game? Or a clever way for the government to track our location?
